# Marketplace Platform for Farmers

Welcome to our Marketplace Platform that connects farmers directly with customers! This platform allows farmers to sell their products, including fresh produce, dairy, and handmade goods, directly to consumers. 

## Features
- **Direct Sales**: Farmers can list their products and manage sales directly without any intermediaries.
- **Chat Feature**: Buyers can communicate directly with sellers to inquire about products, negotiate prices, and arrange deliveries.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: A simple, intuitive layout for both farmers and buyers makes the shopping experience seamless.
- **Secure Transactions**: All transactions are encrypted and secure, providing peace of mind for both parties.

## Getting Started
1. **Sign Up**: Create an account as a farmer or buyer.
2. **List Products**: Farmers can add products to their profile.
3. **Browse Products**: Buyers can search for products by category or location.
4. **Communicate via Chat**: Use the chat feature to ask questions about products or to finalize purchase details.
